INTEGO Y'ISOMERO RYACU

Intego yacu

Intego yacu ni ugutegura abanyeshuri bacu kuba abantu bakiranuka, bafite ubumenyi bw'ingenzi, bakagira umutima wo gufasha abandi, kandi bakaba abayobozi b'intege nke mu muryango, mu rwego rw'igihugu no ku rwego mpuzamahanga.

Aho tugeze

Tugeze mu rwego rwo gutanga uburezi burambye bushobora gufasha umwana wese kubona ubushobozi bwe, gutera imbere mu buzima bwe, no kugira uruhare mu iterambere ry'igihugu.

Icyerekezo cyacu

Kuba isomero ryizewe mu gutanga uburezi bw'igezeho, bukoresha ikoranabuhanga, bukaba bukomeje kwigisha indangagaciro zo kwubahisha Imana, gukunda igihugu, no gukunda abandi nk'uko twikunda ubwacu.

INDANGAGACIRO Z'ISOMERO RYACU

Ukwizera

Dukomeza kwizera Imana no kuyubahisha mu byose dukora

Uburezi

Twizihiza uburezi nk'ingirakamaro mu iterambere ry'umuntu

Ubumwe

Dukora hamwe mu ruhare, mu bwumvane no mu mwizerano

Uburinganire

Twizera ko abantu bose bafite uburenganzira bungana

Ubuntu

Twibanda ku mico y'ubuntu n'urukundo mu mibereho yacu yose
